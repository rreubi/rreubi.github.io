Harry's History

Harry Styles was 16 when he auditioned for the X Factor. Throughout his time on the X Factor, he was put into a group with 4 other boys. Those 5 boys became One Direction.

The band had huge success all over the world. When the band split up in 2015 all the Boys already had a massive fan base and following. 2 years after the bands split Harry released his first album, “Harry Styles”.

After the first album Harry’s fanbase continued to grow, and now there were Harry Styles fans and not just One Direction fans.

In his solo career Harry has now released 3 highly successful albums, has gone on 1 world tour, and many other tours around the UK.

Recently Harry has also started his acting career, performing in Dunkirk, and Eternals, and having leading roles in the movies, My Policeman, and Don’t Worry Darling.

Harry has won 2 Grammys for his newest album, “Harry’s House”, 4 Brit awards, 5 teen choice awards, and many other successes.

Harry Styles is now one of the most popular and successful singers out there. People from all over the world love Harry and his music.
