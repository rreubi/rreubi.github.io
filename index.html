Formation Of The Biggest Boy Band In The World

In 2010 the boy band One Direction was formed on the British singing show The X Factor. The boys started off as single artists, but when they didnâ€™t make it to the next round, the judges decided to put them together and create a boy band. A couple of years later after their formation, One Direction became one of the biggest Bands in the world.

The Band consisted of 5 members. Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson,& Zayn Malik. The boys were ages 16-18 when they started, and they had no idea how big that they were going to get. After their first album, Up All Night, was released it took off immediately.

After the first album was released, and since it was such a big success, the boys went on their first world tour in 2011. And their success kept continuing to grow, and the Directioners' fan base became a worldwide group of lovers for the band.

The boys continued to make and produce music, coming out with 3 more super successful albums.

In 2015, the band had most likely hit the peak of their career They had released 4 albums, and gone on 5 worldwide tours. In early 2015 one member, Zayn Malik, announced his withdrawal from the group to work on his solo career.

After Zayn left the group continued as just the 4 of them and managed to create another top-seller album. Even though the album was a success the band announced their indefinite hiatus in December 2015.
