Louis's History

Louis Tomlinson was 18 when he auditions for the X Factor in 2010.

After the band’s split Louis created and produced a couple of singles which included, “Just Hold On”. “Back To You”, and “Miss You”.

A couple of years later after the band split, Louis released his first album “Walls” where fans instantly fell in love with his solo work.

Through the next couple of years Louis recorded and released a second album titled “Faith in the Future”.

Louis had been on one world tour so far touring his album “Walls” and he recently announced that he is going on another world tour in 2023 to help promote his newest album. Aside from these major world tours, Louis has also toured all over Britain.

In Louis’s personal life he was dating a model, Eleanor Calder for a few years, and together the couple had a son, Freddy.

Louis has also started up his acting career making his first movie appearance in his own documentary. “All Those Voices”. The documentary gives fans an inside view of Louis's life, get the see his thoughts on his solo work, thoughts on One Direction, and behind the scenes of his world tour.

Louis fans are a super strong network and support the singer throughout his entire career, and they are excited for him to release new music in the future.
