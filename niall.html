Niall's History

Niall Horan was 16 when we tried out as a solo artist on the X Factor. Through his time on the X Factor he was grouped with a few other boys to form a boy band. And One Direction went on through the X Factor receiving third place.

While in One Direction, Niall started creating a huge flooring of loving fans. Many Directioners had Niall as their favorite member. So when Niall went solo he had no problem with attracting fans.

Niall released his first solo album, “Flicker”, in late 2017. This album was a huge success and now has over 1 billion streams.

Niall has continued to grow his solo career releasing another album in 2020, and now is working on his third album released in June of 2023.

Now Niall is working on helping other aspiring musicians, being a coach on the TV show The Voice, He is coaching his team to step out of their comfort zones and help advance their voices to become the best singers they can be.

Throughout Niall’s career he has won 2 iHeart music awards, and 3 radio Disney music awards as well.

Niall has become very successful and people all over the world are in love with him, his personality, and of course his music.
